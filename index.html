Controls:

While the mouse is clicked on the viewport and held...

  • WASD to move the camera horzontally, Q/E for down/up.
  • Drag the mouse in the direction you want the camera to turn.
  • Hold either shift to make the camera travel 5x as fast

The frame can be resized by dragging the anchor at the bottom right of the frame or by selecting a preset resolution from the drop-down (a smaller render can boost performance if that is an issue)

Increase the sample count to get a less and less grainy render (although this decreases performance exponentially)
